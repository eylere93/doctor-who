Alex Kingston

Alexandra Elizabeth "Alex" Kingston[1] (born 11 March 1963[1]) is an English actress. She is known for her roles as Dr. Elizabeth Corday on the NBC medical drama ER and as River Song in the BBC science fiction seriesDoctor Who.
